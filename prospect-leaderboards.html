SEAGER — Plate Discipline Composite
Higher is better · Baseball Prospectus proprietary · Min 200 pitches · AAA only
# Player Org Lv Age SEAGER

SEAGER is a Baseball Prospectus plate discipline composite measuring how selectively aggressive a hitter is — it rewards swinging at strikes and laying off balls. The higher the number, the better the eye at the plate.

Average Exit Velocity
Higher is better · Baseball Prospectus · AAA only
# Player Org Lv Age Avg EV (mph)

Average Exit Velocity measures the typical speed of the ball off the bat. Higher EV correlates strongly with hard contact, extra-base hits, and raw power output.

Whiff% — Lowest Swing-and-Miss Rate
Lower is better · Baseball Prospectus · Min 200 pitches · AAA only
# Player Org Lv Age Whiff%

Whiff% is the percentage of swings that result in a miss. Lower is better — elite contact hitters routinely post Whiff% below 15%. MLB average typically sits around 25%.

CStr% — Called Strike Rate
Lower is better · Baseball Prospectus · Min 200 pitches · AAA only
# Player Org Lv Age CStr%

Called Strike% measures how often a batter takes a pitch that is called a strike. Low CStr% indicates a hitter with a sharp zone awareness — he's not getting frozen by strikes he should swing at.

BB% — Walk Rate
Higher is better · Baseball Prospectus · Min 150 PA · AA + AAA
# Player Org Lv Age BB%

Walk Rate is the percentage of plate appearances ending in a walk. It measures patience and zone recognition — elite prospects with 15%+ BB% are forcing pitchers to work around them.

ISO — Isolated Power
Higher is better · FanGraphs · Min 150 PA · AA + AAA
# Player Org Lv Age ISO

Isolated Power (SLG − AVG) strips out singles and measures only extra-base hit production. .200+ is plus power at the minor league level; .250+ is a legitimate power threat.

Smart Aggressor Composite
Sees a good pitch · Swings hard · Does damage  ·  SEAGER 35% + Exit Velo 35% + ISO 30%
# Player Org Lv Age SEAGER Pct Avg EV Pct ISO Pct Score

The Smart Aggressor composite identifies prospects who embody the ideal offensive profile: pitch recognition (SEAGER), contact quality (Exit Velocity), and power output (ISO). Each player is percentile-ranked within the pool, then combined at 35% / 35% / 30%. A score above 75 is elite.
