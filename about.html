Pete Dwyer,
The Baseball Nerd

The Baseball Nerd is an independent baseball media brand covering MLB, MiLB, and all things related to the game, built on the belief that data tells a better story than conventional wisdom ever will.

Founded in 2023, The Baseball Nerd has grown to more than 1,600 subscribers through original analytics work, long-form investigative series, and a commitment to covering the game the way it deserves to be covered. Without the noise. Coverage spans the full professional game, from the major league level down through the minor league system, with a particular focus on the AL West and the Texas Rangers organization.

Pete Dwyer is the author of a published baseball scorekeeping book and how-to scoring guide, available through Amazon. It is a practical resource for fans who want to connect with the game the way it was meant to be followed. He is a member of the IBWAA and an active contributor to various baseball publications.

The Baseball Nerd Analytics Suite provides subscribers with proprietary SPARK Score and FADE Score frameworks, original research tools built from public data, alongside a growing library of datasets and workbooks available in the Data Vault.

This is independent media. No corporate backing, no editorial filters, no agenda beyond getting it right.
